I have a fear of heights, and falling, and falling from heights, so I am not sure why I keep signing myself up for these things.

But as the wise adage goes,

You only live once

The Nevis Swing is a 134-metre pendulum swing suspended above a deep canyon in Queenstown. It’s the highest swing in New Zealand, and also one of the highest in the world.

Are you out of your mind?

That's the question I asked myself when I signed up for the Nevis Swing. But I watched BTS Bon Voyage S4, where the boys went on the swing, and that was it. If they did it, then I am doing it as well! That's the life of a dedicated fangirl.

Signing Up

We booked tickets online on the AJ Hackett website . Since we've already done the Auckland Skywalk, we got a 20% discount - pretty good incentive to keep coming back for more.

On the day, just show up at the location on time, where you'll be weighed and to prompted to sign a waiver form.

Unfortunately, that was how I found out I've gained considerable weight since I first stepped foot in NZ ☹️

The Experience

Once you've put on the harness and crossed the bridge, you will be on the platform where you're supposed to swing from.

The operators will strap you in when it's your turn, ask whether you want to do a countdown or a surprise, and when you pick countdown, they will surprise you anyway.

Honestly, the experience was great fun. You're only scared for 0.5 seconds right when you fall, and then it was pure exhilaration.

This experience shaved off a few years of my life, then doubled it and gave it back.
