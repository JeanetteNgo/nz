About Me

A working holiday, a year of wandering, a blog about both

🧳

[Your Name]

[your nationality] · [job/field] · [age]

I came to New Zealand on a working holiday visa in January 2024, with a vague plan to stay for a few months. Twelve months later I was still here, having worked a handful of jobs, driven most of the country's main roads, hiked fourteen trails, and eaten a truly unreasonable number of meat pies.

This blog is my attempt to document it honestly — not the Instagram version of travel, but the actual one. The getting-lost bits, the hard bits, the bits where the mountain is so beautiful you can't talk for a moment.

Year in Review
Mar 2024
Arrived in Christchurch
First stop in NZ. Settled in, explored, got a feel of the city. Dealt with admin, planned our next route, bought a car.
Apr 2024
Haast
A housekeeping stint that evolved into a web design assignment. Got my first taste of hiking in NZ.
May 2024
Nelson
Stayed with a local mountain biking coach and his family. Did video editing. Scrumptious meals everyday.
Kaikoura
My favourite town hands down! Did whale watching, seal kayaking, hiked my first mountain.
Jun - Sep 2024
Hanmer Springs
Undertook another web/brand design project. Enjoyed the Hot Springs so much we became regulars :) Did a bungy jump.
Sep 2024
Methven
Signed up for ski lessons. Woke up to views of Mt Hutt everyday.
Oct 2024
Kaiapoi
Worked on a local family farm. Weeded strawberry beds, planted flower seeds, gazed at grazing lambs. Dyed my hair blue.
Oct - Nov 2024
Trip with Friends
Went on a 2-week trip with friends. Checked off all the tourist spots: Lake Tekapo, Mount Cook, Milford Sound, Queenstown, Wanaka.

Made our way to the North Island. Got into my first car accident. Sorted things out and continued our journey through Wellington and on to Auckland.
Nov - Dec 2024
Whangārei
Stayed with the loveliest landlady while the car was getting fixed. Took the chance to explore Northland. Made the trip to Cape Reinga. Went to the local farmer's market. Had the best strawberries of my life.

Tried snorkeling, parasailing, canyoning, sandboarding, surfing.
Jan 2025
Home
Left from Auckland. Cried a small amount at the gate. Started writing this blog on the flight back.
