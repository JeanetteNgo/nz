Edoras

I contemplated visting Mt Sunday when I was staying at Methven, but never did wake up early enough to make the trip before work. Big regret! It's a ~1h 10min drive one-way from Methven, but 2h+ from Christchurch. 😩 I was approaching the end of my work holiday and didn't want to leave with any regrets, so I made the drive nonetheless. It was all grey, cloudy and drizzly when I left Christchurch at 1pm. Weather was not looking good, and the traffic wasn't any better ://

I must have been blessed, because by the time I reached Mt Sunday and began the trek up at 3.30pm, the weather had cleared up and it was vast beautiful blue skies, accompanied by fat white puffy clouds in the distance. My definition of perfect weather! 🥹

The trek itself is short and easy - it only takes about 30min to reach the top and the payoff is enormous. You get unparalleled 360° views of the surrounding mountains and plains. Apparently, many tour groups visit in the day (this is a relatively accessible film location for LOTR fans), but since it was getting pretty late, there weren't many people around.

I found a quiet spot to sit with my snacks, and in that moment, life felt absolutely perfect. This remains one of my favourite moments in NZ, partly because I'm so proud of myself for making the 4h solo drive (and braving the 40min stretch of gravel road...) given how anxious I get about driving.

Here's to buying a house at Methven, getting my own Ford Ranger, and tailing someone else's ass down the gravel road to Mt Sunday every weekend! 🤞 Jk... or am I.
